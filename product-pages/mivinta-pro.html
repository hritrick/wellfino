Product Description

MIVINTA PRO contains whey protein, DHA, multivitamins, and multiminerals. It boosts immunity, strengthens the nervous system, and supports bone health.

Ideal for pregnancy, lactation, children, and post-illness recovery. Available in two delicious flavors:

  • Kesar Elaichi: A unique traditional Indian flavor with saffron, perfect for those who prefer aromatic taste profiles.
  • Chocolate: A classic flavor that makes it an enjoyable addition to daily routines, whether consumed as a meal replacement or nutritional supplement.

Each serving provides essential nutrients to support overall health and wellness, making it an excellent choice for those with increased nutritional needs.
