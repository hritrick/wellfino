Product Description

K2-NIVCAL is a specialized bone health supplement combining Vitamin K2-7 (100 mcg) and Calcium Citrate (500 mg) in a synergistic formula designed to optimize calcium utilization in the body.

Vitamin K2-7 directs calcium to bones rather than soft tissues, preventing arterial calcification while enhancing bone density. This makes K2-NIVCAL particularly beneficial for individuals with osteoporosis, osteopenia, and those at risk of bone density loss.

Key Benefits:

  • Enhances calcium absorption and utilization
  • Directs calcium to bones where it's needed
  • Reduces risk of arterial calcification
  • Supports overall bone health and strength
  • Helps prevent osteoporosis and bone density loss

Composition

Ingredient Strength
Vitamin K2-7 100 mcg
Calcium Citrate 500 mg

Suggested Use:

Take one tablet daily with a meal, or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Note: Individuals on blood-thinning medications should consult a healthcare provider before use. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
