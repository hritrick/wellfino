Product Description

Laxomyn is a gentle yet effective laxative designed to provide reliable relief from constipation. This carefully formulated medication works by increasing water content in the intestines and stimulating natural bowel movements without causing discomfort or dependency.

2 Capsules with lukewarm water provide optimal relief for occasional constipation, helping to restore regular bowel function and alleviate associated symptoms like bloating and discomfort. Laxomyn is suitable for adults experiencing constipation due to dietary changes, certain medications, or changes in routine, and works gently overnight for morning relief.
