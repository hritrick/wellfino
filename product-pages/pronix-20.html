Product Description

Pronix-20 is a high-potency, multi-strain probiotic supplement formulated to support digestive health, strengthen immune function, and promote overall gut balance. Each capsule delivers a robust dose of 60 Billion CFU, combining three clinically studied probiotic strains.

Probiotic Strains:

Streptococcus thermophilus (20 Billion CFU): Known for its role in lactose digestion and gut flora stabilization, this strain helps maintain intestinal integrity and supports immune modulation.
Lactobacillus acidophilus (20 Billion CFU): A well-documented probiotic that supports the breakdown of food, enhances nutrient absorption, and maintains a healthy balance of intestinal microflora.
Bifidobacterium longum (20 Billion CFU): A beneficial bacteria that aids in reducing inflammation, enhancing immune response, and improving bowel regularity.

Pronix-20 is designed for daily use to promote gastrointestinal health, restore natural gut flora disrupted by antibiotics, and support optimal digestive function. It is suitable for adults seeking reliable gut health support.

Key Benefits:

  • Supports digestive and intestinal health
  • Enhances immune system response
  • Aids in restoring healthy gut flora balance
  • Helps relieve symptoms of bloating and irregularity

Suggested Use:

Take one capsule daily with or without food, or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Note: This product is free from preservatives, gluten, and artificial additives. Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. For best results, refrigerate after opening.
