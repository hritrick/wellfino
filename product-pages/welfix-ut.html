Product Description

WELFIX-UT is a comprehensive urinary tract health syrup formulated with cranberry extract, D-mannose, and a blend of herbs traditionally used to support urinary tract health. This unique formula helps maintain urinary tract health, provides relief from UTI symptoms, and supports the body's natural defense mechanisms.

Key Ingredients:

Cranberry Extract: Contains proanthocyanidins that prevent bacteria from adhering to the urinary tract walls, reducing the risk of infection.
D-Mannose: A naturally occurring sugar that helps flush bacteria from the urinary tract by preventing bacterial adhesion to urinary tract walls.
Uva Ursi Extract: Contains arbutin, which has antimicrobial properties that help combat urinary tract infections.
Dandelion Extract: Acts as a natural diuretic, increasing urine production and helping to flush out bacteria.

Key Benefits:

  • Helps prevent and manage urinary tract infections
  • Reduces burning sensation during urination
  • Supports healthy urinary flow
  • Maintains urinary tract health
  • Provides antioxidant support
  • Pleasant taste for improved compliance

Suggested Use:

Adults: Take 10ml (2 teaspoons) twice daily after meals, or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Children (6-12 years): Take 5ml (1 teaspoon) twice daily after meals, or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Note: Shake well before use. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.

Caution: Consult a physician before use if pregnant, nursing, or under medical supervision. If symptoms persist for more than 48 hours, consult a healthcare professional.
