Product Description

Xitcid is a specialized proton pump inhibitor (PPI) designed to effectively control and reduce excessive stomach acid production. Each tablet contains 40mg of Pantaprazole, a potent acid-suppressing agent that works by blocking the enzyme system responsible for acid secretion in the stomach lining.

This medication provides extended relief from acid-related conditions including gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), erosive esophagitis, and Zollinger-Ellison syndrome. Xitcid is particularly effective for patients with persistent heartburn, acid regurgitation, and stomach discomfort caused by excessive acid. The once-daily dosing regimen offers convenience while maintaining powerful 24-hour acid control to promote healing of damaged esophageal tissue and prevent recurrence of symptoms.
