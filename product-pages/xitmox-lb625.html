Product Description

XITMOX-LB625 is a powerful combination of antibiotic (Amoxycillin 500 mg with Clavulanic Acid 125 mg) and probiotic (Lactic Acid Bacillus, 60 million spores) that treats a wide range of bacterial infections while preventing antibiotic-associated diarrhea.

Indicated for LRTI, URTI, SSTI, UTI, dental and gynecological infections, this balanced formulation provides effective antimicrobial action while supporting gut health. The addition of Clavulanic Acid enhances efficacy against beta-lactamase producing bacteria, while the probiotic component helps maintain beneficial intestinal flora, reducing the risk of digestive side effects common with antibiotic therapy.
