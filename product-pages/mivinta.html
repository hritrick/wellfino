Product Description

MIVINTA is a nutritional supplement with Lycopene, Green tea extract, and essential nutrients. It is indicated for cardiovascular disease, arthritis, pernicious anemia, oxidative stress, and chronic fatigue.

MIVINTA promotes overall health, immunity, and vitality. This balanced formula provides key vitamins and minerals to support optimal functioning and help address nutritional deficiencies.
